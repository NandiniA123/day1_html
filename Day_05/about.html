MOTHER THERESA

MTCA at glance Mother Theresa Institute of computer applications was established during the academic year 2002-2003. This has been approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Sri Venkateswara University, Tirupathi, Andhra Pradesh. Within a short span of time it has become a reputed institution due to its academic Excellency.The college was established with the sole objective of providing quality education to the rural students.The institution is managed by Sri M. Ravindra Babu,an young and dynamic Philanthropist, who Posses excellent managerial skills and administrative capabilities. Mother Theresa Institute of Computer Applications serves as an educational hub to students of Rural Polytechnic, and Degree Colleges to pursue higher education. College building is constructed on a sprawling 2.4 acres campus of elevated land surrounded by pollution free lush green scenic vegetation. It is well connected by road. It is 130Kms away from Bangalore (Silicon) city, and 45Kms from Chittoor, on Chittoor Bangalore National highway. The climate is very similar to that of Bangalore. Nearest airports are Bangalore and Chennai.
